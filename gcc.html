GCC Services We Make a Difference

In today's global economy, expanding your footprint is key to growth, but it can be a costly and complex challenge, especially for small and medium-sized businesses. At Kaaptureminds, we specialize in helping organizations establish and scale Global Capability Centers (GCCs) in India—a world-renowned hub for skilled and cost-effective IT talent. We provide a low-risk, high-reward pathway to building a strategic extension of your business. Our solutions are designed to deliver not just efficiency and cost savings, but also the flexibility you need to succeed in a multi-country environment.

Our End-to-End GCC Service Models

We offer flexible engagement models that reduce operational complexities and accelerate your time to market.

Build-Operate-Transfer (BOT)

Our flagship Build-Operate-Transfer (BOT) model is the perfect solution for businesses seeking a risk-free path to establishing a fully owned and managed center. We handle all the complexities of the setup, allowing you to focus on your core business.

  • Build: We manage every aspect of the initial setup, including legal entity registration, office space selection, infrastructure setup, and technology provisioning.

  • Operate: For a pre-defined period, we operate the center for you, managing all day-to-day activities, from HR and payroll to IT support and performance management. This ensures your center runs at peak efficiency from day one.

  • Transfer: Once the center is stable, fully operational, and meets all pre-agreed KPIs, we seamlessly transfer full ownership and control to your company.

Our Shared Services Support

We help you centralize and streamline critical business functions, enabling significant cost reductions and improved control.

  • Talent Acquisition & Management: Leveraging our deep knowledge of the Indian talent market, we offer Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO) with SLA-driven hiring. We source, screen, and onboard top-tier talent for a range of roles, from niche technical skills to administrative and support functions.

  • Human Capital Services: We provide comprehensive support from employee onboarding to separation, including payroll management, benefits administration, and compliance with local labor laws.

  • Finance & Accounting: Streamline your financial operations with our dedicated shared services. This includes master data management, invoice processing, asset management, collections, reconciliations, and payment processing, all of which are managed with the highest standards of accuracy and security.

Why Partner with Kaaptureminds?

  • Cost-Effectiveness: We enable you to tap into India's vast and highly skilled talent pool, reducing your operational and labor costs by up to 40% compared to Western locations.

  • Risk Mitigation: Our models absorb the initial risks associated with foreign expansion, including legal, compliance, and infrastructure investments.

  • Speed to Market: We leverage our established network and expertise to accelerate your launch, helping you begin operations and realize value faster than if you were to go it alone.

  • Scalability: Whether you need a small, specialized team or a large, multi-function center, our solutions are designed to scale with your business needs.

Whether you are setting up your first GCC or looking to expand your existing operations globally, we ensure your center delivers measurable business outcomes while maintaining strategic alignment with your goals.
