Oracle & Cloud Services

Oracle and Cloud Services

At Kaaptureminds, we're dedicated to helping businesses transform their operations with leading Oracle technologies. We specialize in Oracle Fusion Applications and Oracle E-Business Suite, offering end-to-end services that ensure you get maximum value from your ERP investments. We don't just implement systems; we architect solutions for your business.

Oracle E-Business Suite (EBS) Services

Our team of seasoned experts provides comprehensive services for Oracle EBS, ensuring your on-premise ERP remains a powerful and stable asset. We help you unlock new efficiencies and maintain peak performance.

  • Implementations, Upgrades, and Rollouts: From new module implementations to seamless upgrades (including the latest R12.2 version) and global rollouts, we ensure a smooth transition with minimal disruption.
  • Managed Services: We offer proactive support, maintenance, and system monitoring to keep your EBS environment running efficiently, allowing your internal teams to focus on strategic initiatives.
  • Performance Tuning and Enhancements: We analyse and optimize your system's performance, applying patches, custom extensions (CEMLIs), and modernizations to enhance functionality and user experience.

Oracle Fusion Cloud Services

Harness the power of the cloud with our expertise in Oracle Fusion Applications. We guide you through every step of your cloud journey, from strategy to deployment and beyond.

  • Cloud Migrations: We specialize in moving your business from Oracle EBS or other legacy systems to Oracle Fusion Cloud. Our proven methodologies minimize risk and downtime, ensuring a seamless transition of your data, processes, and applications.
  • New Implementations: We implement new Oracle Fusion Cloud instances from the ground up, tailored to your specific business requirements across key pillars like ERP (Financials, Procurement), SCM (Supply Chain Management), and HCM (Human Capital Management).
  • Integrations: We build robust integrations to connect Oracle Fusion with other on-premise or cloud applications using Oracle Integration Cloud (OIC). This ensures a unified and seamless flow of data across your entire enterprise ecosystem.

Specialized Offerings

  • Testing Center of Excellence: Our dedicated team provides both manual and automated testing for ERP systems. We leverage modern testing frameworks to ensure the quality, reliability, and security of your Oracle applications.
  • Rapid Deployment Frameworks: We utilize accelerators and pre-configured templates to reduce implementation time and cost, enabling faster adoption and improved time-to-value.
  • AI and Intelligent Automation: We embed AI and automation into Oracle ERP workflows, including AI-driven agents for customer service and RPA for streamlined tasks, boosting productivity and decision-making.
