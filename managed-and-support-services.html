Oracle Managed and Support Services

Oracle Managed Services

Reliable and affordable managed support for your Oracle applications

Running business-critical Oracle applications is not a one-time project — it’s an ongoing journey that demands stability, agility, and proactive management. At Kaapture Minds, we understand that Oracle E-Business Suite and Oracle Fusion Cloud are the backbone of your finance, HR, and supply chain operations.

That’s why our Managed Services go beyond simple support. We provide SLA-driven services for incident resolution, enhancements, monitoring, and optimization — all supported by a strong governance framework and flexible engagement models.

And let’s be honest — is an Oracle implementation ever truly finished? ERP systems must constantly adapt to evolving business needs. Whether driven by customer expectations, global expansion, or the push for internal process improvements, your Oracle ERP must keep up with change.

Common Challenges We See

  • Lack of a dedicated managed services partner who can deliver on SLA-driven support.
  • Difficulty keeping up with Oracle’s continuous enhancements, patches, and regulatory updates.
  • Risk of business disruption due to insufficient testing or reliance on manual scripts.
  • Pressure on internal teams managing daily support and innovation simultaneously.

How Kaapture Minds Solves This

  • Dedicated Oracle Expertise: Specialized managed services team ensuring consistent support, predictable SLAs, and deep product knowledge.
  • Automation-First Approach: Leveraging test automation and proactive monitoring to reduce manual effort and improve accuracy.
  • Innovation with Stability: Ensuring compliance and stability while adopting new Oracle capabilities to make ERP a driver of innovation.

With Kaapture Minds as your Oracle Managed Services partner, you not only protect and stabilize your ERP investment but continuously improve it. We free up your teams to focus on strategic priorities while ensuring your Oracle systems remain secure, agile, and future-ready.

Oracle Test Automation

Smarter, faster, and reliable testing for Oracle Cloud and E-Business Suite

Testing is one of the most critical activities in maintaining reliable Oracle applications — yet it’s often the most resource-intensive and overlooked. Every quarterly patch, new rollout, or entity setup brings the risk of disruption if testing is rushed or incomplete.

At Kaapture Minds, we simplify this with Oracle-focused test automation frameworks tailored for Financials, HCM, and Supply Chain. Our solutions reduce testing effort by up to 50%, accelerate release cycles, and ensure your systems remain compliant and business-ready.

Current Challenges with Oracle Testing

  • Quarterly Oracle updates require repetitive regression testing, overwhelming teams.
  • Manual testing during rollouts is slow, error-prone, and delays go-live timelines.
  • Balancing speed of delivery with quality and compliance assurance is challenging.

How Kaapture Minds Solves This

  • Automation-Driven Testing: Reusable, script-based frameworks that cut manual effort by up to 50%.
  • End-to-End Regression Coverage: Comprehensive validation across Financials, HCM, and SCM during upgrades.
  • Accelerated Quarterly Testing: Ready-to-execute regression packs minimize downtime and business risk.
  • Agile Enablement: Automating repetitive testing tasks to let teams focus on innovation.

With Kaapture Minds as your Test Automation partner, you gain faster testing cycles, fewer errors, and the confidence to embrace Oracle enhancements without disruption.
